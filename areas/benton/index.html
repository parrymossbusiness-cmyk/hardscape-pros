Paver patios, retaining walls, and outdoor living construction serving Benton, Saline County.

Serving Benton

Benton is the Saline County seat southwest of Little Rock along I-30, and it's one of the fastest-growing communities in the metro as families move out from the city while staying within easy commuting distance. That growth means a lot of our Benton projects are new construction lots where the builder's original grading needs correcting, alongside plenty of established neighborhoods near downtown Benton and the Saline River with older hardscape that's due for repair or replacement.

Saline County's soil carries the same dense clay common across central Arkansas, and Benton's terrain along the Saline River corridor adds another factor: low-lying lots near the river and its tributaries need extra attention to grading and drainage to keep water away from patios, retaining walls, and foundations during heavy spring rain. We assess river proximity and existing drainage patterns as part of every Benton estimate.

Retaining walls are common on Benton's newer subdivision lots, where builders often leave a raw graded slope between properties that homeowners later want turned into usable, level yard space. We build those walls with proper drainage from the start, along with paver and stamped concrete patios, driveways, and outdoor living structures across the city.

Outdoor kitchens and pergolas have also become popular requests as Benton's newer neighborhoods fill in with larger lots that have room for a full outdoor living space. We plan those builds around the same base and drainage standards we apply to every project, so a pergola or kitchen island isn't sitting on a patio that shifts or cracks within a few years.
