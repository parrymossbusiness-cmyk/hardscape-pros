Serving Little Rock

Little Rock is home base for Hardscape Pros, and it's the city where we've built the largest share of our paver patios, retaining walls, and outdoor living projects. As the seat of Pulaski County and the state capital, Little Rock spans everything from the rolling, wooded hillsides of the Heights and Chenal Valley to flatter, older neighborhoods closer to the Arkansas River, and the hardscape approach that works well in one part of town often needs adjusting in another.

That variation is exactly why we treat every Little Rock estimate as its own project rather than a template. Hillside lots in western Little Rock frequently need retaining walls and drainage work before a patio or outdoor kitchen can even go in, while flatter properties closer to downtown are more likely to need grading corrections to move water away from a foundation. Across nearly all of it, the underlying soil is a dense central Arkansas clay that expands when saturated and shrinks as it dries, which is the single biggest reason hardscapes fail here when base prep and drainage aren't done correctly the first time.

We compact every base in lifts, install drainage behind retaining walls and beneath patios where grade requires it, and grade finished hardscape so water moves away from your home instead of pooling against it — the same standard whether we're building a paver patio in Hillcrest, a retaining wall in the Heights, or an outdoor kitchen in Chenal Valley. Little Rock's freeze-thaw winters, while milder than points further north, still put real stress on poorly based concrete and pavers, so we never skip base depth to save a day of labor.

Hardscape Pros serves all of Little Rock and neighbors it closely with North Little Rock, Sherwood, Maumelle, and Alexander. If you're planning a patio, retaining wall, outdoor kitchen, or driveway project anywhere in the city, we'll walk your property and provide a free, written estimate before any work begins.
