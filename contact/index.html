About Hardscape Pros

Hardscape Pros is a locally owned hardscape and patio construction company based in Little Rock, Arkansas, serving Little Rock, North Little Rock, Conway, Benton, Sherwood, Bryant, Maumelle, Jacksonville, Cabot, Alexander, Mayflower, and Searcy. We build paver patios, retaining walls, outdoor living structures, and driveways engineered for central Arkansas clay soil and freeze-thaw cycles.
