FAQ

Common Questions About Hardscape Projects

Do you serve my area?+

Yes — we serve the entire Little Rock metro, including North Little Rock, Conway, Benton, Sherwood, Bryant, Maumelle, Jacksonville, Cabot, Alexander, Mayflower, and Searcy. If you're within about 30 minutes of downtown Little Rock, we've likely already built something nearby. Call or text (501) 555-0134 to confirm your address.

Are you licensed and insured?+

Yes. Hardscape Pros is fully licensed and carries general liability insurance on every job, from a single walkway to a full outdoor living build. We'll provide proof of coverage before work begins so you can install with confidence.

How long does a typical patio or retaining wall project take?+

Most paver patios and standard retaining walls take between 3 and 10 working days once excavation starts, depending on size, base depth, and drainage requirements. Larger outdoor living builds with kitchens or pergolas can run two to four weeks. We give you a written timeline at the on-site assessment, not a rough guess.

What affects the cost of a hardscape project?+

The biggest cost drivers are square footage, material choice (paver vs. stamped concrete vs. natural stone), site access, existing grade and drainage, and how much excavation and base material the soil requires. Central Arkansas clay often needs deeper base prep than sandier soils, which affects cost but prevents heaving and settling later. We give itemized, free estimates so you know exactly what you're paying for.

Do I need a permit for a retaining wall or patio installation in Little Rock?+

It depends on wall height and project scope — many retaining walls over a certain height and some patios attached to drainage changes require a permit from the local building department. We handle the permit process on projects that need one and will tell you upfront during your free estimate whether yours applies.
